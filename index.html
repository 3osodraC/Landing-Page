Improve your efficiency with [pomodoro]!
[pomodoro] helps you increase your work and study efficiency by taking short breaks from time to time. Sign up to learn more!

The technique:

Set a timer for 25 minutes.
Do whatever needs to be done.
When the timer finishes, take a 5-10 minute break, and go back to work.
Do this 3 times, and take a 20-30 minute break the 4th time.
